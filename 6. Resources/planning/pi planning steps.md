# PI-56 Planning Steps

This document outlines the specific, actionable steps required to successfully navigate the PI-56 planning cycle for Synapse and MCG Everywhere.

## Step 1: Initiative Definition (Due: Apr 30, 2026)
Identify and document the strategic initiatives for the platform section of Synapse for the 2027 fiscal year.

- [ ] **Review "MCG Everywhere" Roadmap**: Ensure initiatives align with the overarching goal of agentic AI.
- [ ] **Draft Key Initiatives**: Populate the Confluence page with 2-3 major outcomes (not just features).
- [ ] **Update Budget Workbook**: Enter preliminary product investment asks into the "2027 Product Portfolio Priorities" SharePoint file.
- [ ] **Synchronize with Peter Owen**: Ensure platform asks are consistent with the Analytic KT items discussed on Monday 4/20.

## Step 2: SEAD Counterpart Alignment (Due: Before May 5)
PI planning is a "two-in-a-box" effort between Product and Engineering.

- [ ] **Confirm SEAD Dev Manager**: Identify the engineering counterpart for the platform area.
- [ ] **Schedule 1:1 Workbook Sync**: Review current and proposed initiatives to ensure technical viability.
- [ ] **Populate Prioritization Workbook**: Add Synapse-specific rows to the PI-56 Workbook in Confluence.

## Step 3: PI-56 Prioritization Session (Tue May 5, 2026)
Prepare to defend and rank initiatives during the live meeting.

- [ ] **Prepare RICE/Weighted Scoring**: Have a justification ready for the Impact and Reach of each initiative.
- [ ] **Identify Dependencies**: Flag any cross-product dependencies (e.g., if Synapse needs a core CARE engine update).
- [ ] **Update Workbook Post-Meeting**: Refine the list based on team feedback and trade-offs made during the session.

## Step 4: Leadership Product Review (Due: May 8, 2026)
Convert the planned initiatives into a "Leadership-Ready" view.

- [ ] **Finalize Strategic Narrative**: Summarize how these initiatives move the needle on "Accelerated Market Traction."
- [ ] **Review with Paru Srinivasan**: Ensure alignment before the CARE & SEAD leadership sync.
- [ ] **Address Executive Feedback**: Log any "Landmines" or redirects encountered during the session.

## Step 5: Engineering Estimation (Due: May 31, 2026)
Hand off finalized priorities for technical sizing.

- [ ] **Provide Clarification**: Be available for the SEAD team to ask "What does X actually mean?" questions.
- [ ] **Monitor Estimation Progress**: Ensure the SEAD team stays on track for the end-of-month deadline.
- [ ] **Final Review**: Validate that the estimates match the expected scope.
