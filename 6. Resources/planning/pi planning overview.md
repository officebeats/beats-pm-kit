# PI-56 Planning Overview

## Strategic Context: MCG Everywhere & Synapse Strategy
The PI-56 planning cycle marks a critical transition in how MCG Everywhere and Synapse are prioritized. As discussed in the recent MCG Strategy sessions, the focus is shifting away from legacy "mechanics" towards an **AI-first operating mode**. This cycle is designed to be asynchronous and low-friction, focusing on demonstrating value at early adopters (Clover Health, Telligen) and accelerating market traction with agentic AI workflows.

### Core Objectives for PI-56
1. **Demonstrate Value**: Establish a clean ROI story through Synapse results at two key customers.
2. **AI-First Operating Mode**: Bring new capabilities to market (e.g., ODG product demo) within a single quarter.
3. **Agentic AI Mobilization**: Transition from LLM-as-a-tool to agentic orchestrators that can measure business impact directly.
